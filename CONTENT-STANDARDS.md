# Content Standards

Voice is clear, practical, trustworthy, and locally useful. Write Armenian-first content with Russian and English compatibility. Avoid hype; explain product value, price differences, merchant trust, delivery, availability, and next actions plainly.

Content hierarchy should support scanning: concise headings, short paragraphs, bullet lists for specs, strong price labels, and consistent CTA text. Product and category copy must be factual, avoid unsupported claims, and be useful for SEO without keyword stuffing.

Use consistent terms for products, stores/merchants, offers, availability, discounts, reviews, comparison, and CTAs. Do not place localizable text inside images. Important legal, price, availability, and merchant-source caveats must be visible near the related action.
