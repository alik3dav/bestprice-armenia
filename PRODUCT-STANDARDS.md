# Product Marketplace Standards

BestPrice Armenia is a price-comparison marketplace. Product experiences must optimize discovery, comparison, trust, and conversion.

Product cards must show image, title, rating/review count when available, price, merchant/store count or availability, and optional discount/favorite/compare actions. Product detail pages must surface title, media, best price, store comparison, availability, merchant CTA, specs, reviews, related products, and SEO content in a clear order.

Offer comparison rows emphasize store, price, delivery/availability, trust signal, and CTA. Best-price or sponsored labels must be explicit. Category and search pages require breadcrumbs, title, product count, sort, selected filter chips, product grid, and mobile filter access.

Prices must use AMD formatting consistently. Product data must distinguish unknown, unavailable, out-of-stock, and active offers.
