# Conversion Standards

Conversion work must improve user decision speed without reducing trust. CTAs must be consistent, visible, localized, and tied to a clear destination. Use urgency or discount messaging only when supported by data.

Above-the-fold product pages must answer: what is it, what is the best price, where can I buy it, is it available, can I trust the merchant, and what should I do next. Category pages must keep products visible quickly; SEO copy and promotions must not bury grids.

Measure conversion changes through click paths, comparison row clarity, filter usage, product-card readability, and mobile CTA reachability. Sponsored placements must be labeled and must not masquerade as organic best-price results.
