# SEO Standards

Every public route must preserve or improve SEO. Use unique metadata titles/descriptions, canonical URLs where needed, crawlable server-rendered content for product/category/search landing pages, and stable localized slugs.

Use one clear `h1` per page, hierarchical headings, descriptive link text, alt text for meaningful images, and internal links between categories, products, merchants, and related items. Product and merchant pages should use schema.org structured data when data is available. Category pages should include concise crawlable descriptions without pushing products too far down.

URLs should be lowercase, stable, human-readable, and avoid leaking implementation details. Sitemaps and robots rules must include indexable public marketplace pages and exclude admin/auth/private areas. Pagination, filters, and sorting must avoid duplicate-content traps through canonical and indexability decisions.
